Creamy Garlic Parmesan Pasta

Ingredients :

  • 250g pasta
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 4 cloves garlic. minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta and drain.
  2. Melt butter and saute garlic.
  3. Add cream and simmer.
  4. Mix in parmesan cheese.
  5. Combine with pasta and serve hot.

Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 eggs
  • 50g Parmesan cheese
  • Black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Boil the pasta.
  2. Fry pancetta until crispy.
  3. Mix eggs and cheese in a bowl.
  4. Combine pasta with pancetta then stir in egg-cheese mixture off heat.
  5. Season and serve.

Chicken Biryani

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 onion (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • Biryani masala, garlic-ginger paste, salt, oil

Instructions:

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, spices, and garlic-ginger paste for 1 hour.
  2. Fry onion until golden, then add tomatoes and marinated chicken, cook well.
  3. Boil rice separately until 80% cooked.
  4. Layer rice and chicken in a pot, cover and steam for 20 minutes.
  5. Serve with raita or salad.

Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 and 1/2 cups all-purpose
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease a round cake pan.
  2. Mix flour, sugar, cocoa, baking soda, and salt in a bowl.
  3. In another bowl, mix water, oil, vinegar, and vanilla.
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients. Mix until smooth.
  5. Pour into the cake pan and bake for 30-35 minutes.
  6. Let cool before serving. Optionally top with chocolate glaze or frosting.

Vegan Buddha Bowl

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa or brown rice
  • 1 cup roasted sweet potatoes (cubed)
  • 1/2 cup cooked chickpeas
  • 1/2 avocado (sliced)
  • 1/2 cup sautéed kale or spinach
  • 1/4 cup shredded carrots
  • 2 tbsp hummus or tahini dressing

Instructions:

  1. Roast sweet potatoes with olive oil, salt, and pepper at 200°C (400°F) for 20–25 minutes.
  2. Prepare quinoa or rice according to package instructions.
  3. Assemble all ingredients in a bowl: base of grains, then add veggies, chickpeas, and avocado.
  4. Top with hummus or drizzle with tahini dressing.
  5. Serve warm or chilled.

Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 1 tbsp butter

Instructions:

  1. Butter one side of each bread slice.
  2. Place cheese between the unbuttered sides of the bread slices.
  3. Heat a pan over medium heat. Place sandwich in pan, buttered side down.
  4. Cook until golden brown, about 2-3 minutes per side, flipping once.
  5. Serve hot with soup or ketchup.
